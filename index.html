My Projects

Movies Correlatoin

The project aimed to explore the relationship between movie budget, votes and gross earnings using a dataset of over 7000 movies. Through data cleaning, visualization and correlation analysis, the project found that there is a strong positive linear relationship between budget and gross, and a moderate relationship between votes and gross. In other words, movies with bigger budgets tend to have higher gross earnings, and movies with more votes tend to have higher gross earnings as well. However, it's important to note that the success of a movie may be influenced by a variety of different factors. Overall, the project provides insights into the factors that may contribute to a movie's financial success.
